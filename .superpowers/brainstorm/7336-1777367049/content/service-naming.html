A — Technical / Engineering-led

Precision language. "Engineering" over "Development", "Architecture" over "Building". Appeals to technical founders and CTOs who want to know you understand the depth of what they're asking for.

B — Outcome / Solution-led

Names signal what you'll have at the end, not how it's built. "DeFi Protocol Development" tells the founder the deliverable. "Autonomous AI Agents" tells them the outcome. Easier to scan for non-technical stakeholders.

C — Hybrid: Name + Subtitle (Recommended)

Clean service name + a short 4–6 word descriptor in muted text. Founders scan the name, technical stakeholders read the subtitle. Works in the dropdown and on hub pages. Eliminates ambiguity without being verbose.
